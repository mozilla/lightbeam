Keep browsing the web. As you do so, a graph on this page will change. Each dot represents a website that has interacted with your browser.

Sites in red are confirmed trackers by privacychoice.org that are currently tracking you. Sites in gray are not confirmed trackers, but set a cookie and may still track you. Sites in blue did not set a cookie, but a request was sent from the visited page to them.

Sites with a glowing halo are sites that you have visited. Sites without a halo are sites you have not visited.

Hover your mouse over the dots to learn more about them.
