VISUALIZATION
Graph View
BEST for
Seeing site relationships
DESCRIPTION

In this graph visualization, you can see circular nodes that are websites you have visited, triangular nodes are third party site and connections between them. Over time, this graph will become a network of connections and nodes to visualize your relationship with the web.

This graph may get complex quickly and to reduce the amount of info displayed use SESSIONS to help focus on a set of data.

FEATURES
Zoom In + Out - on scroll
Pan - on click + drag
Tooltips - on hover
Website Profile - on click
LEGENDS AND CONTROLS

These buttons serve as toggles to help identify various elements on the graph.

When you set your site preferecnes:

  • Cookies means the site stored some data in your browser.
  • Block means they are blocked from connecting to your browser.
  • Watch means they are highlighted.

Visualization Help
VISUALIZATION
Clock View
BEST for
Seeing site patterns with time
DESCRIPTION

In this clock visualization, you can see circular nodes in green represents websites you have visited and yellow nodes that are sites you have not but still connected to you and the connections between them. Over time, this view reveals patterns across the arch that represent your browsing behavirous according to time and your relationship with the web.

This visualization becomes populated as 15 minutes increments on the arc and the previous (24 hour) session is seen faded to compare data from the previous day.

FEATURES
Zoom In + Out - on scroll
Pan - on click + drag
Tooltips - on hover
Website Profile - on click
LEGENDS AND CONTROLS

These buttons serve as toggles to help identify various elements on the graph.

When you set your site preferecnes:

  • Block means they are blocked from connecting to your browser.
  • Watch means they are highlighted.

List View
BEST for
Seeing a text database
DESCRIPTION

In this list visualization, you can see al ist of all websites you have visited and sites you have not visited but still connect to you. This view assists with sorting and filtering large numbers of sites.

This list may get quite extensive and to reduce the amount of information displayed use SESSIONS to help you focus on a set list of data.

FEATURES

SORT BY - Type, Preference, WEbsite(A-Z), Date

When you set your site preferecnes:

  • Block means they are blocked from connecting to your browser.
  • Hide means you have the option to hide them from visualization.
  • Watch means they are highlighted.

ABOUT THIS ADD-ON

Collusion is an experimental add-on for Firefox that allows you to see which sites are using third-party cookies to track your movements across the Web. It shows, in realtime, how that data creates a spiderwed of interaction between companies and other trackers.
